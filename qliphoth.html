The Qliphoth

The shells of the Tree. Ten counter-departments. The inverse of every sephirah — not evil in opposition to good, but each divine quality taken to its pathological extreme. Entropy administered, not merely opposed.

The Qliphothic tree is a campaign-long reveal. The Troppers working within it believe — correctly — that they serve the will of the One. The coordination between Thagirion and the Solar Compact is the central twist. Withhold this page from players until the appropriate moment.

Qliphoth

Each qliphah mirrors a sephirah. The purple dot marks the secret coordination between Thagirion and Tiphareth's Solar Compact.
